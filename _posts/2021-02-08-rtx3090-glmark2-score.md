I just obtained one of the elusive RTX 3090s (nerd flex).
In particular, I obtained a [Gigabyte RTX 3090](https://www.gigabyte.com/Graphics-Card/GV-N3090GAMING-OC-24GD), pictured below.

![](https://www.gigabyte.com/FileUpload/Global/KeyFeature/1624/innergigabyteimages/kf-img.png)
